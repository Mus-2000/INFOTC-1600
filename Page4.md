**Adding effects to vocal tracks in Logic Pro X**

Logic Pro X comes with a lot of stock plugins and effects that can help you manipulate the recording.

If you want to explore these effects, press I on the keybaord or navigate to the Inspector button and click on it.

<img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2021-12-01 at 7 36 41 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54383601/144341800-4c79a1ab-7be4-44a8-845a-b2b7ce3eab42.png">


After that navigate to the section that says Audio Fx and click on to display the menu of effects.

<img width="1440" alt="Screen Shot 2021-12-01 at 7 36 49 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/54383601/144341859-75702574-9f1e-4c5c-9ac7-92d8337e1e40.png">

 This is what the drop-down menu of effects looks like. There is a lot of options to choose from and a lot can be done with these stock plugins provided by Logic Pro X.
